Beef Stir Fry

**Beef Stir-Fry with Broccoli and Carrots:** **Ingredients:** - 1 pound beef, thinly sliced - 2 cups broccoli florets - 1 cup sliced carrots - 2 tablespoons soy sauce - 1 tablespoon oyster sauce - 1 tablespoon cornstarch - 1 teaspoon sugar - 1 teaspoon sesame oil - 2 tablespoons vegetable oil - 2 cloves garlic, minced - 1 teaspoon ginger, grated - Rice for serving **Instructions:** 1. In a bowl, mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, cornstarch, sugar, and sesame oil. Add sliced beef and marinate for at least 15 minutes. 2. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or skillet over high heat. 3. Stir-fry marinated beef until browned. Remove from the wok and set aside. 4. In the same wok, add a bit more oil if needed. Stir-fry garlic and ginger until fragrant. 5. Add broccoli and carrots to the wok. Stir-fry until vegetables are crisp-tender. 6. Return the cooked beef to the wok. Stir to combine and heat through. 7. Serve over rice. Enjoy your Beef Stir-Fry with Broccoli and Carrots!
